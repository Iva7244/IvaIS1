6 Thinking Hats

White Hat

Red Hat

My partner said that the idea made them feel good and was excited thinking about how the changes could help the cupboard.

Black Hat

My partner questioned if I'm truly putting the user first when making these changes.

Yellow Hat

Green Hat

My partner suggested interviewing regular users of the cupboard and asking the staff about common issues they face.

Blue Hat

My partner said I should gather more information from those experienced with the comet cupboard to narrow down my problem statement.
