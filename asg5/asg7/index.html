Biography

Kronk is a henchman to Yzma, the advisor of current Incan emperor Kuzco. He has a very athletic build and is kind and hospitable to all. Although he may lack some common sense he makes up for it with his big heart.

Why He Would Be a Good Ruler

Kronk has the ability to scale mountains and carry extremely heavy weight. He also is caring towards anyone despite their background, and even has the ability to communicate with animals. His intense strength and morality would make him a powerful but also fair leader.

Campaign Platform

Stricter Food Inspection Laws: Kronk will make sure that food and drinks can't be easily poisoned or potioned. He has seen the consequences of using potions for evil and because of this is adiment about keeping citizen's food safe. He also has strong culinary skills, making him more equipped to detect issues with food.
