Things to Do

Nan Ao Island

Nan Ao island is off the Shantou coast and has beautiful warm weather year-round with a breeze in the summer. There are many mountains to scale containing diverse flora and fauna. Nan Ao also has oppurtunities to explore it's history through the County Museam of Cultural Relics and Historic Sites

Symbolic Tower of the Tropic of Cancer

Shantou Small Park

This park has many historical buildings, unique food stalls, and perfect photo-op areas. An iconic building includes the red pavillion.
