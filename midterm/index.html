Shantou

Shantou is a city in the Chinese province, Guangdong. It has historically been home to Teochew people, a southern Chinese ethnic group with a rich culture.

Fun Facts

  • Shantou is one of the seven special economic zones in China
  • Teochew opera is popular in the city and is often accompanied by religous and ancestral ceremonies
  • One of Shantou's biggest industries is toy manufacturing
  • The number of Teochew dialect speakers is shrinking as more young residents of Shantou learn Mandarin instead
