Restaurants to Eat at

Chao Lin Fu Restaurant

This restaurant is a great place to try traditional chaozhou and teochew dishes. They have great seafood options and follows the lost cooking style of the late historical chef Lin Ziran.

Jianye Restaurant

Jianye Restaurant is one of the oldest in Shantou and is affordable for tourists compared to bigger cities. It is also one of the best places to have goose, a delicacy in the city.

XingXing Restaurant

This restaurant is famous for it's Cantonese inspired take on fried gaoke. Fried gaoke is a fried rice cake seasoned with fish sauce, red soybean sauce, and cooked with shrimps and oysters in the pan.
