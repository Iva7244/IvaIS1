Rainbow

Bio

Rainbow (also known as Ritchie Blackmore's Rainbow or Blackmore's Rainbow) were an English rock band formed in Hertford in 1975 by guitarist Ritchie Blackmore. They originally featured four members of the American rock band Elf, including their singer Ronnie James Dio

They are my favorite because of how their early albums helped shaped the metal genre and fantasy themed music. Blackmore's epic guitar riffs combined with Dio's powerful vocal performances and mystical lyrics created some of the most beautiful metal albums of their era.
