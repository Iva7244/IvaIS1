Storytelling forces you to really understand your audience. Designers can transfer their research in an anecdotal way, which allows them to better empathize with the user and recall information more easily. Storytelling also gives designers the ability to define the scope of their project before prototyping, like when the team for Yahoo Personal was better able to understand the function of their website after using storytelling in their ideation process.
